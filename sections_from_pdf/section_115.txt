[F23127AAApplication of sections 28 to 34 in Wales
[
F232(1)]
In relation to land in Wales, sections 28 to 34 (which relate to sites of special scientific
interest and limestone pavements) have effect as if references to Natural England were
references to [F233the Natural Resources Body for Wales and as if section 28D(2)(d)
were omitted].
[
F234(2)
Subsection (3) applies where—
(a)
any provision of sections 28 to 34 requires the Natural Resources Body for
Wales to give a notification or notice to the local planning authority in whose
area land is situated, and
(b)
all or part of the land is included in a strategic planning area designated under
section 60D of the Planning and Compulsory Purchase Act 2004.
(3) The Natural Resources Body for Wales must also give the notification or notice to the
strategic planning panel for the strategic planning area.]]
