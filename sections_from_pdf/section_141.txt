[F31128S Notices and signs relating to SSSIs
(1) Natural England may, on any land included in a site of special scientific interest, put
up and maintain notices or signs relating to the site.
(2) Natural England may remove any notice or sign put up under subsection (1).
(3) Any other person who intentionally or recklessly and without reasonable excuse takes
down, damages, destroys or obscures a notice or sign put up under subsection (1) is
guilty of an offence.
(4) A person guilty of an offence under this section is liable on summary conviction to a
fine not exceeding level 4 on the standard scale.]
