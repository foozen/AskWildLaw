[F28028H Statutory undertakers, etc.: duty in relation to carrying out operations.
(1) A section 28G authority shall give notice to [F281Natural England] before carrying out,
in the exercise of its functions, operations likely to damage any of the flora, fauna or
geological or physiographical features by reason of which a site of special scientific
interest is of special interest.
(2) Subsection (1) applies even if the operations would not take place on land included
in a site of special scientific interest.
